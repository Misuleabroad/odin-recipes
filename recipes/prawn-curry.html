Prawn curry

Description

Indian prawn curry. Unbelievably easy to make and lip-smacking, this prawn curry is one of my all-time favorites. The aromatic spices give it a wonderful flavor, and the prawns are cooked to perfection in the delicious sauce.
Spoon this fragrant curry over a bed of fluffy rice or pair it with your favorite classic flatbread for an effortless meal that’s made for cooler months. If you love prawns, make this Indian prawn curry, a classic featuring the beautiful spices and luscious coconut milk. Subtly spiced with rich and flavorful sauce, this recipe is your best friend when it comes to making delicious weeknight meal in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 1cm Fresh Ginger, Peeled and Chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Peeled and Chopped
  • 1 Green Chilli, Chopped
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric
  • 1 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1 Tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 Tsp Garam Masala
  • 500g Prawns
  • Water
  • 1 Can Coconut Milk
  • 2 Tbs Chopped Coriander Leaves
  • Steamed Rice to Serve
  • Roti or Naan to serve

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add onions. Sauté, stirring occasionally, for 2-3 minutes or until they are soft and translucent.
  2. Add ginger, garlic and green chilies (if using). Sauté, stirring, for 1 minute or until fragrant.
  3. Add ground turmeric, ground cumin, ground coriander, garam masala and red chili powder. Sauté, stirring, for a minute or until aromatic.
  4. Add tomato paste. Sauté, stirring, for few seconds.
  5. Add prawns and 1 cup water. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, and simmer, covered, for 5-10 minutes depending on the size of the prawns or until they are cooked through.
  7. Add coconut milk. Simmer for 1 minute.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  9. Serve with rice or naan.
